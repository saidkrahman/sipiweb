    Annual Picnic Department of Electrical Technology

    The grand annual picnic for the esteemed Department of Electrical Technology

    The grand annual picnic for the esteemed Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) at Shyamoli Ideal Polytechnic Institute was joyously held in the picturesque coastal town of Cox's Bazar, nestled on the southeastern shores of Bangladesh. On the auspicious date of February 13th, 2024, all faculty members enthusiastically partook in the festivities, marking yet another memorable occasion of camaraderie and relaxation amidst the breathtaking natural beauty of Cox's Bazar.
