    Welcome to Shyamoli Ideal Polytechnic Institute (SIPI): Your Gateway to Technical Education Excellence!

    Shyamoli Ideal Polytechnic Institute (SIPI) is a renowned institution, established in 1979 under the Bangladesh Technical Education Board, dedicated to delivering top-tier technical education. As a leading polytechnic institute in Dhaka, Chittagong, Rangpur, and Laxmipur, SIPI has consistently achieved remarkable results, cementing its position as a top educational choice in Bangladesh.

    Our mission at SIPI is to equip students with modern, information technology-based, career-oriented education, creating opportunities for self-employment and career advancement. With a commitment to co-educational, career-oriented modern education, we nurture self-reliant personalities and instill a sense of humanity in our students, preparing them for successful careers and responsible citizenship.

    At SIPI, we offer comprehensive support, including comfortable hostel facilities for both male and female students, a robust transportation network, and a dedicated Placement Center. Our experienced faculty, with a minimum of 10 years of practical teaching and industrial experience, ensures that you receive world-class technical education.

    SIPI goes beyond shaping careers; we nurture futures. Join us on this journey of educational excellence and self-discovery. We believe in fostering a brighter and more prosperous tomorrow for our students and our society.

    Choose SIPI for a future filled with opportunities and success in the world of technical education. Welcome to Shyamoli Ideal Polytechnic Institute!
